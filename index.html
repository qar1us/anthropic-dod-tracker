On March 3, 2026, the U.S. Department of War designated Anthropic — an American company — a supply chain risk to national security. A label historically reserved for foreign adversaries like Huawei and ZTE. The designation followed Anthropic's refusal to allow Claude to be used for mass domestic surveillance or fully autonomous weapons without human oversight. This is a record of who responded — and who stayed silent.
